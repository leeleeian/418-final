Parallel Limit Order Book Simulation

Lillian Yu + [Partner Name]

Summary

We build a parallel simulation of a limit order book to study how synchronization strategies impact scalability under realistic financial workloads.

Background

A limit order book processes buy and sell orders using price-time priority. Matching operations depend on shared global state, making parallelization challenging.

The Challenge

The system must maintain strict ordering while supporting concurrent updates. We explore contention, synchronization overhead, and workload skew.

Approach

  • Baseline: single-threaded engine
  • Coarse-grained locking
  • Fine-grained locking
  • Batching strategies
